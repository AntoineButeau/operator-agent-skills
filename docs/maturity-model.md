# Maturity Model

## draft
Structurally valid and useful for internal trials, but not yet reviewed through repeated real use.

## usable
Validated structurally and sampled for coherence. Suitable for routine internal work with the stated human review gates.

## reviewed
Tested across multiple realistic scenarios, edited for specificity, and reviewed by the relevant domain owner.

## publishable
Ready for external release after confidentiality, brand, legal, and safety review. Public examples and source links are clean.
