# Conflict Without Damage Pack

## Source series
Conflict Without Damage

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to convert conflict without damage material into operator-grade decisions and reusable artifacts. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [Conflict Pattern Diagnosis](skills/conflict-pattern-diagnosis.md) — handle the system step without overlapping the other skills in the pack.
2. [Clean Disagreement Brief](skills/clean-disagreement-brief.md) — produce a reusable decision brief or operating spec.
3. [Repair Conversation Plan](skills/repair-conversation-plan.md) — turn the diagnosis into an executable plan with owners and gates.
4. [Conflict Escalation Check](skills/conflict-escalation-check.md) — handle the system step without overlapping the other skills in the pack.
