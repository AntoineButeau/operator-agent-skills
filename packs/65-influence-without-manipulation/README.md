# Ethical Influence Pack

## Source series
Influence Without Manipulation

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to convert influence without manipulation material into operator-grade decisions and reusable artifacts. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [Influence Intent Check](skills/influence-intent-check.md) — handle the system step without overlapping the other skills in the pack.
2. [Stakeholder Motivation Map](skills/stakeholder-motivation-map.md) — make the system visible by mapping owners, flows, and evidence.
3. [Ethical Persuasion Brief](skills/ethical-persuasion-brief.md) — produce a reusable decision brief or operating spec.
4. [Manipulation Risk Review](skills/manipulation-risk-review.md) — diagnose the current state and name the operating risk.
