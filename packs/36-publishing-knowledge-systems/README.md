# Publishing Pipeline Pack

## Source series
Publishing & Knowledge Systems

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to convert publishing & knowledge systems material into operator-grade decisions and reusable artifacts. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [Idea to Draft Pipeline](skills/idea-to-draft-pipeline.md) — handle the pipeline step without overlapping the other skills in the pack.
2. [Editorial Calendar Builder](skills/editorial-calendar-builder.md) — produce a reusable artifact or operating spec.
3. [Knowledge Asset Inventory](skills/knowledge-asset-inventory.md) — make the system visible by mapping owners, flows, and evidence.
4. [Publishing Quality Gate](skills/publishing-quality-gate.md) — handle the gate step without overlapping the other skills in the pack.
