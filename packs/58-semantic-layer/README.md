# Semantic Layer Pack

## Source series
The Semantic Layer: Where Business Meaning Becomes Infrastructure

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to convert the semantic layer: where business meaning becomes infrastructure material into operator-grade decisions and reusable artifacts. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [Business Term Definition Map](skills/business-term-definition-map.md) — make the system visible by mapping owners, flows, and evidence.
2. [Metric Semantic Audit](skills/metric-semantic-audit.md) — find gaps between stated process and observed behavior.
3. [Semantic Layer Change Plan](skills/semantic-layer-change-plan.md) — turn the diagnosis into an executable plan with owners and gates.
4. [Meaning to Infrastructure Brief](skills/meaning-to-infrastructure-brief.md) — produce a reusable decision brief or operating spec.
