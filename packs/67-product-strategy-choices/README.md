# Product Strategy Choices Pack

## Source series
Product Strategy That Actually Makes Choices

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to convert product strategy that actually makes choices material into operator-grade decisions and reusable artifacts. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [Product Choice Map](skills/product-choice-map.md) — make the system visible by mapping owners, flows, and evidence.
2. [Roadmap Tradeoff Brief](skills/roadmap-tradeoff-brief.md) — produce a reusable decision brief or operating spec.
3. [Strategy Fit Review](skills/strategy-fit-review.md) — diagnose the current state and name the operating risk.
4. [Choice Communication Plan](skills/choice-communication-plan.md) — turn the diagnosis into an executable plan with owners and gates.
