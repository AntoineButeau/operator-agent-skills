# Market Expansion Pack

## Source series
Internationalization & International Go-To-Market

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to convert internationalization & international go-to-market material into operator-grade decisions and reusable artifacts. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [Market Selection Scorecard](skills/market-selection-scorecard.md) — make the system visible by mapping owners, flows, and evidence.
2. [Localization vs Adaptation Check](skills/localization-vs-adaptation-check.md) — diagnose the current state and name the operating risk.
3. [International GTM Readiness](skills/international-gtm-readiness.md) — handle the readiness step without overlapping the other skills in the pack.
4. [Market Entry Plan](skills/market-entry-plan.md) — turn the decision into sequenced work with gates and owners.
