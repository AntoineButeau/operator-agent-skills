# Pricing Decision Pack

## Source series
Pricing and Packaging

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to convert pricing and packaging material into operator-grade decisions and reusable artifacts. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [Pricing Segmentation Brief](skills/pricing-segmentation-brief.md) — compress the situation into a decision-ready brief.
2. [Price Metric Evaluator](skills/price-metric-evaluator.md) — handle the evaluator step without overlapping the other skills in the pack.
3. [Discount Signal Analysis](skills/discount-signal-analysis.md) — handle the analysis step without overlapping the other skills in the pack.
4. [Pricing Change Rollout](skills/pricing-change-rollout.md) — turn the decision into sequenced work with gates and owners.
