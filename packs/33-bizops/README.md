# BizOps Decision Support Pack

## Source series
BizOps / Business Operations

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to convert bizops / business operations material into operator-grade decisions and reusable artifacts. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [BizOps Problem Framing](skills/bizops-problem-framing.md) — handle the framing step without overlapping the other skills in the pack.
2. [Decision Support Model Spec](skills/decision-support-model-spec.md) — produce a reusable artifact or operating spec.
3. [Operational Deep Dive Plan](skills/operational-deep-dive-plan.md) — turn the decision into sequenced work with gates and owners.
4. [Recommendation Memo Builder](skills/recommendation-memo-builder.md) — produce a reusable artifact or operating spec.
