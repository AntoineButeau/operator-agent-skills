# Platform Strategy Pack

## Source series
Platform Strategy

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to convert platform strategy material into operator-grade decisions and reusable artifacts. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [Platform Boundary Map](skills/platform-boundary-map.md) — make the system visible by mapping owners, flows, and evidence.
2. [Ecosystem Incentive Review](skills/ecosystem-incentive-review.md) — diagnose the current state and name the operating risk.
3. [Platform Investment Brief](skills/platform-investment-brief.md) — produce a reusable decision brief or operating spec.
4. [Platform Governance Plan](skills/platform-governance-plan.md) — turn the diagnosis into an executable plan with owners and gates.
