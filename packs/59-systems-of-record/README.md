# Systems of Record Pack

## Source series
Systems of Record: Who Owns the Truth?

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to convert systems of record: who owns the truth? material into operator-grade decisions and reusable artifacts. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [Source of Truth Map](skills/source-of-truth-map.md) — make the system visible by mapping owners, flows, and evidence.
2. [Record Ownership Review](skills/record-ownership-review.md) — diagnose the current state and name the operating risk.
3. [Truth Conflict Resolution Plan](skills/truth-conflict-resolution-plan.md) — turn the diagnosis into an executable plan with owners and gates.
4. [System of Record Change Brief](skills/system-of-record-change-brief.md) — produce a reusable decision brief or operating spec.
