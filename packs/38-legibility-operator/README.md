# Legibility Operator Pack

## Source series
Legibility and Illegibility for Operators

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, and AI agents helping those people turn ambiguous work into decisions, systems, and reusable artifacts.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack when the work relates to legibility and illegibility for operators and needs an operator-grade workflow rather than a long-form essay. The skills are designed for practical diagnosis, planning, review, and artifact generation.
