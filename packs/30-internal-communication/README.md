# Company Signal Pack

## Source series
Internal Communication That Actually Works: How Companies Move Context, Decisions, and Signal Without Drowning in Noise

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to convert internal communication that actually works: how companies move context, decisions, and signal without drowning in noise material into operator-grade decisions and reusable artifacts. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [Company Signal Brief](skills/company-signal-brief.md) — compress the situation into a decision-ready brief.
2. [Decision Log Entry](skills/decision-log-entry.md) — handle the entry step without overlapping the other skills in the pack.
3. [Communication Channel Audit](skills/communication-channel-audit.md) — diagnose the current state and name the operating risk.
4. [Context Package Builder](skills/context-package-builder.md) — produce a reusable artifact or operating spec.
