# AI Work Design Pack

## Source series
Work Design for the AI Era

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to convert work design for the ai era material into operator-grade decisions and reusable artifacts. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [AI Work Allocation Map](skills/ai-work-allocation-map.md) — make the system visible by mapping owners, flows, and evidence.
2. [Human-Agent Handoff Design](skills/human-agent-handoff-design.md) — design the operating artifact, interface, or workflow contract.
3. [Role Redesign Brief](skills/role-redesign-brief.md) — produce a reusable decision brief or operating spec.
4. [Work Design Risk Review](skills/work-design-risk-review.md) — diagnose the current state and name the operating risk.
