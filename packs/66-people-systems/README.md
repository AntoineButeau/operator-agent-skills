# People Systems Pack

## Source series
People Systems That Actually Raise the Bar

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to convert people systems that actually raise the bar material into operator-grade decisions and reusable artifacts. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [Talent Bar Definition](skills/talent-bar-definition.md) — handle the system step without overlapping the other skills in the pack.
2. [Hiring Signal Scorecard](skills/hiring-signal-scorecard.md) — define evaluation criteria and evidence standards.
3. [Performance System Review](skills/performance-system-review.md) — diagnose the current state and name the operating risk.
4. [Promotion Calibration Brief](skills/promotion-calibration-brief.md) — produce a reusable decision brief or operating spec.
