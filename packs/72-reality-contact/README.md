# Reality Contact Pack

## Source series
Reality Contact: Escaping the Introspection Trap

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to convert reality contact: escaping the introspection trap material into operator-grade decisions and reusable artifacts. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [Introspection Trap Diagnosis](skills/introspection-trap-diagnosis.md) — handle the system step without overlapping the other skills in the pack.
2. [External Signal Plan](skills/external-signal-plan.md) — turn the diagnosis into an executable plan with owners and gates.
3. [Belief Update Review](skills/belief-update-review.md) — diagnose the current state and name the operating risk.
4. [Reality Contact Cadence](skills/reality-contact-cadence.md) — handle the system step without overlapping the other skills in the pack.
