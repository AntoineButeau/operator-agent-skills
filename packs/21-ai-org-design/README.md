# AI Org Design Pack

## Source series
Operating Structure for the AI Era

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to convert operating structure for the ai era material into operator-grade decisions and reusable artifacts. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [AI-Era Role Redesign](skills/ai-era-role-redesign.md) — produce a reusable artifact or operating spec.
2. [Agent-Human Boundary Map](skills/agent-human-boundary-map.md) — make the system visible by mapping owners, flows, and evidence.
3. [AI Governance Cadence](skills/ai-governance-cadence.md) — handle the cadence step without overlapping the other skills in the pack.
4. [Org Capability Shift Plan](skills/org-capability-shift-plan.md) — turn the decision into sequenced work with gates and owners.
