# Forward-Deployed Company Pack

## Source series
The Forward-Deployed Company

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to convert the forward-deployed company material into operator-grade decisions and reusable artifacts. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [Field Operating Model Map](skills/field-operating-model-map.md) — make the system visible by mapping owners, flows, and evidence.
2. [Embedded Team Scope Brief](skills/embedded-team-scope-brief.md) — produce a reusable decision brief or operating spec.
3. [Customer Feedback Loop Design](skills/customer-feedback-loop-design.md) — design the operating artifact, interface, or workflow contract.
4. [Deployment Risk Review](skills/deployment-risk-review.md) — diagnose the current state and name the operating risk.
