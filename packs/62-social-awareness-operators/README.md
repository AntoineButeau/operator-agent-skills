# Social Awareness Operator Pack

## Source series
Social Awareness for Operators

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to convert social awareness for operators material into operator-grade decisions and reusable artifacts. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [Room Read Map](skills/room-read-map.md) — make the system visible by mapping owners, flows, and evidence.
2. [Stakeholder Empathy Brief](skills/stakeholder-empathy-brief.md) — produce a reusable decision brief or operating spec.
3. [Social Signal Risk Scan](skills/social-signal-risk-scan.md) — surface risks, constraints, and missing evidence before action.
4. [Meeting Dynamics Review](skills/meeting-dynamics-review.md) — diagnose the current state and name the operating risk.
