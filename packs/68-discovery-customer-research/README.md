# Discovery Research Pack

## Source series
Discovery and Customer Research Without Theater

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to convert discovery and customer research without theater material into operator-grade decisions and reusable artifacts. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [Research Question Framing](skills/research-question-framing.md) — handle the system step without overlapping the other skills in the pack.
2. [Customer Signal Synthesis](skills/customer-signal-synthesis.md) — handle the system step without overlapping the other skills in the pack.
3. [Discovery Theater Audit](skills/discovery-theater-audit.md) — find gaps between stated process and observed behavior.
4. [Research to Decision Brief](skills/research-to-decision-brief.md) — produce a reusable decision brief or operating spec.
