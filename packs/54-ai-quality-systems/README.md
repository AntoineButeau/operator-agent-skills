# AI Quality Systems Pack

## Source series
AI Quality Systems

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to convert ai quality systems material into operator-grade decisions and reusable artifacts. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [AI Quality Bar Definition](skills/ai-quality-bar-definition.md) — handle the system step without overlapping the other skills in the pack.
2. [Eval Suite Builder](skills/eval-suite-builder.md) — handle the system step without overlapping the other skills in the pack.
3. [Model Behavior Drift Review](skills/model-behavior-drift-review.md) — diagnose the current state and name the operating risk.
4. [Quality Incident Response Plan](skills/quality-incident-response-plan.md) — turn the diagnosis into an executable plan with owners and gates.
