# Product Operations Pack

## Source series
Product Operations That Actually Helps Product Teams Ship

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to convert product operations that actually helps product teams ship material into operator-grade decisions and reusable artifacts. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [Product Ops Intake Map](skills/product-ops-intake-map.md) — make the system visible by mapping owners, flows, and evidence.
2. [Launch Readiness System](skills/launch-readiness-system.md) — define the repeatable operating system and cadence.
3. [Product Feedback Routing](skills/product-feedback-routing.md) — handle the system step without overlapping the other skills in the pack.
4. [Product Team Operating Review](skills/product-team-operating-review.md) — diagnose the current state and name the operating risk.
