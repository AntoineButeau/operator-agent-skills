# AI Transformation Pack

## Source series
The AI-Augmented Company

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to convert the ai-augmented company material into operator-grade decisions and reusable artifacts. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [AI Capability Map](skills/ai-capability-map.md) — make the system visible by mapping owners, flows, and evidence.
2. [Team AI Readiness Review](skills/team-ai-readiness-review.md) — diagnose the current state and name the operating risk.
3. [AI Operating Policy Draft](skills/ai-operating-policy-draft.md) — handle the draft step without overlapping the other skills in the pack.
4. [AI Transformation Roadmap](skills/ai-transformation-roadmap.md) — make the system visible by mapping owners, flows, and evidence.
