# AI-Era FinOps Pack

## Source series
FinOps for the AI Era

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to convert finops for the ai era material into operator-grade decisions and reusable artifacts. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [AI Cost Visibility Map](skills/ai-cost-visibility-map.md) — make the system visible by mapping owners, flows, and evidence.
2. [Model Spend Governance Brief](skills/model-spend-governance-brief.md) — produce a reusable decision brief or operating spec.
3. [Unit Economics Scenario Review](skills/unit-economics-scenario-review.md) — diagnose the current state and name the operating risk.
4. [Optimization Backlog Builder](skills/optimization-backlog-builder.md) — handle the system step without overlapping the other skills in the pack.
