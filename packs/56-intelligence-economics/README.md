# Intelligence Economics Pack

## Source series
The Economics of Intelligence

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to convert the economics of intelligence material into operator-grade decisions and reusable artifacts. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [Intelligence Cost Curve Map](skills/intelligence-cost-curve-map.md) — make the system visible by mapping owners, flows, and evidence.
2. [Build Buy Model Routing Brief](skills/build-buy-model-routing-brief.md) — produce a reusable decision brief or operating spec.
3. [Automation ROI Sensitivity Review](skills/automation-roi-sensitivity-review.md) — diagnose the current state and name the operating risk.
4. [Intelligence Allocation Plan](skills/intelligence-allocation-plan.md) — turn the diagnosis into an executable plan with owners and gates.
