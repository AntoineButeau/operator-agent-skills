# Change Adoption Pack

## Source series
Change That Actually Takes: How Companies Move From Announcement to Adoption

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, and AI agents helping those people turn ambiguous work into decisions, systems, and reusable artifacts.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack when the work relates to change that actually takes: how companies move from announcement to adoption and needs an operator-grade workflow rather than a long-form essay. The skills are designed for practical diagnosis, planning, review, and artifact generation.
