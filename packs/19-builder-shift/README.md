# Internal Builder Pack

## Source series
The Builder Shift

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to convert the builder shift material into operator-grade decisions and reusable artifacts. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [Internal Tool Opportunity Map](skills/internal-tool-opportunity-map.md) — make the system visible by mapping owners, flows, and evidence.
2. [Operator to Builder Spec](skills/operator-to-builder-spec.md) — produce a reusable artifact or operating spec.
3. [Prototype Scope Cutter](skills/prototype-scope-cutter.md) — handle the cutter step without overlapping the other skills in the pack.
4. [Builder Adoption Plan](skills/builder-adoption-plan.md) — produce a reusable artifact or operating spec.
