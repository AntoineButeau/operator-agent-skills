# AI Adoption Pack

## Source series
AI Adoption That Actually Changes the Company

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to convert ai adoption that actually changes the company material into operator-grade decisions and reusable artifacts. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [AI Adoption Readiness Map](skills/ai-adoption-readiness-map.md) — make the system visible by mapping owners, flows, and evidence.
2. [Behavior Change Plan](skills/behavior-change-plan.md) — turn the diagnosis into an executable plan with owners and gates.
3. [Adoption Friction Diagnosis](skills/adoption-friction-diagnosis.md) — handle the system step without overlapping the other skills in the pack.
4. [Adoption Measurement System](skills/adoption-measurement-system.md) — define the repeatable operating system and cadence.
