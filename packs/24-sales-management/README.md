# Sales Manager OS Pack

## Source series
Sales Management That Actually Works

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to tighten forecast calls, pipeline inspection, and rep coaching into one sales manager operating system. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [Pipeline Inspection Routine](skills/pipeline-inspection-routine.md) — produce a reusable artifact or operating spec.
2. [Rep Coaching Plan](skills/rep-coaching-plan.md) — turn the decision into sequenced work with gates and owners.
3. [Forecast Call Prep](skills/forecast-call-prep.md) — handle the prep step without overlapping the other skills in the pack.
4. [Sales Operating Cadence](skills/sales-operating-cadence.md) — handle the cadence step without overlapping the other skills in the pack.
