# GTM Strategy Pack

## Source series
Go-To-Market Strategy

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to translate market thesis into ICP focus, channel choices, and account-plan work. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [GTM Thesis Builder](skills/gtm-thesis-builder.md) — produce a reusable artifact or operating spec.
2. [ICP Focus Check](skills/icp-focus-check.md) — diagnose the current state and name the operating risk.
3. [Channel Fit Evaluator](skills/channel-fit-evaluator.md) — handle the evaluator step without overlapping the other skills in the pack.
4. [GTM Operating Plan](skills/gtm-operating-plan.md) — turn the decision into sequenced work with gates and owners.
