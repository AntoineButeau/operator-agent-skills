# Individual Operator Pack

## Source series
From Individual Contributor to Individual Operator

## Who it is for
Operators, founders, executives, managers, chiefs of staff, BizOps leads, product leaders, revenue leaders, and AI agents helping them make decisions from live operating context.

## When to use this pack
Use this pack to convert from individual contributor to individual operator material into operator-grade decisions and reusable artifacts. The four skills are deliberately sequenced so diagnosis, mapping, planning, and review do not collapse into one vague prompt.

## Progression / use order
1. [Operator Scope Map](skills/operator-scope-map.md) — make the system visible by mapping owners, flows, and evidence.
2. [Work Legibility Update](skills/work-legibility-update.md) — compress the situation into a decision-ready brief.
3. [Autonomy Readiness Plan](skills/autonomy-readiness-plan.md) — turn the decision into sequenced work with gates and owners.
4. [Operator Retrospective](skills/operator-retrospective.md) — produce a reusable artifact or operating spec.
